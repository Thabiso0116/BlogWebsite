What type of media is News24?

News24 is an English-language South African news website created in October 1998 by the multinational media company, Naspers.

Who owns News24?

News24 is owned by Media24, a South African media company, with interests in digital media and services, newspapers, magazines, e-commerce, book publishing, print and distribution.

What is the purpose of news24?

News24 is Southern Africa and Africa's premier online news resource, with round-the-clock coverage to bring you local and international news as it happens, when it happens. The main news sections are South Africa, Africa, World, Sport, Sci-Tech and Entertainment.
